Kimchi Tofu Soup

This is a simple and easy kimchi tofu soup recipe. I was in a phase where I was into soup but as always I don't want to spend all day in the kitchen at the same time still get satisfying and nutrient packed meal to last me all day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 3 cups tofu, cut into cubes
  • 4 cups of water
  • 250g ham, cubed
  • 1 broth cube
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 spoon of sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. 1. In a pot, add water and broth cube. Bring to a boil.
  2. 2. In a fry pan, add oil and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. 3. Add ham and cook until slightly browned.
  4. 4. Add kimchi and sugar, cook for 2-3 minutes until it has caramelized a bit.
  5. 5. Add the sautéed ingredients to the boiling water.
  6. 6. Add soy sauce and tofu cubes to the pot. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  7. 7. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds.
