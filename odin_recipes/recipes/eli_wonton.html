Eli's Wonton Recipe

...is a recipe we came up with from recipes we've seen online. We had to make a few changes to make it our own. We were on a couple months health recovery period and needed to make something we could prep in batches then freeze. We either fry it or make a soup. Hope this helps people in similar situation or when you want something quick and low effort!

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 whole cabbage
  • 3 small red bell peppers
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 4g pack of seasoning
  • 2 packs of wonton wrappers

Instructions

  1. 1. Cut carrots, cabbage, and bell peppers.
  2. 2. Mix all ingredients together in a food processor.
  3. 3. Place a small amount of the mixture in the center of a wonton wrapper.
  4. 4. Fold the wrapper in half and seal the edges with water.
  5. 5. Repeat until all the mixture is used up.
  6. 6. You can either fry the wontons or boil them for soup.

Frying Instructions

  1. 1. Heat oil in a pan over low heat.
  2. 2. Add wontons to the pan, add a little bit of water, and cover.
  3. 3. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the wontons are golden brown and crispy.

Soup Instructions

  1. 1. Boil water in a pot or rice cooker.
  2. 2. Add wontons to the boiling water and cook for about 5 minutes or until they float to the surface.
  3. 3. You can add vegetables or broth to make it a complete meal.
  4. *You can broth cubes for additional flavor.
