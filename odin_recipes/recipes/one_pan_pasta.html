One Pan Pasta

A quick and low effort one pan pasta recipe. Perfect for day's where you are on autopilot but still has to feed the family!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg pasta
  • 1/2 kg ground pork
  • 250g all purpose cream
  • 150g tomato paste
  • 250g water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. 1. In a pan, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. 2. Add ground pork and cook until browned.
  3. 3. Add tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes or until the color turned from bright red to darker.
  4. 4. Add water and bring to a boil.
  5. 5. Add pasta and cook for 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  6. 6. Add salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. 7. Stir in the all purpose cream until melted and well combined.
